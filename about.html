About Us

Welcome to DashupData. We are dedicated to providing modern teams with a powerful, no-code platform to transform raw CSV data into actionable, AI-powered dashboards instantly.

Founded with the mission to democratize data analytics, we believe everyone should be able to visualize, analyze, and gain strategic insights from their data without needing a degree in data science or coding skills.

Our platform handles everything from automated parsing and chart generation to advanced anomaly detection, helping you make data-driven decisions faster.
