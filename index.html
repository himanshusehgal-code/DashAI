Q1: Is my data secure?

Q2: I see a "ScriptError: Authorization is required" or the app is stuck. What should I do?

This usually happens when there is a conflict with old permissions. Follow these steps:
1. Go to your Google Account Permissions.
2. Find "AI SaaS Dashboard" (or your Project Name) and click Remove Access.
3. Come back to the Web App and Reload (Refresh) the page.
4. The app will ask for permissions again. Click "Allow" and it will start working perfectly.
